# Content and asset gaps

Required from the business or Pretix content team:

- A project-first image for every workshop and product, with meaningful Arabic and English alt text.
- Arabic and English “what you will make” copy for every workshop.
- Structured category, skill level, age group, language and duration metadata.
- Verified instructor names, biographies and portraits; none should be inferred.
- Materials included, what to bring, agenda, safety/accessibility notes, parking and branch details.
- Market-specific cancellation, rescheduling, delivery, tax and privacy policies.
- Workshop-to-kit and next-level mappings.
- Consent-backed participant creation images and verified reviews.
- Membership pricing and benefits by market.
- Gift fulfilment and redemption rules.
- Private/corporate enquiry recipients and service-level expectations.

Until supplied, the UI shows neutral fallbacks or omits the section. It must not invent dates, seats remaining, reviews, instructors, policies or savings.
